Company AI Policy — Internal Knowledge Base
==========================================

Section 1: Data Handling
-------------------------
All customer data processed by AI systems must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
Personal Identifiable Information (PII) must never be sent directly to external LLM APIs.
Use anonymization or tokenization before processing sensitive data.

Section 2: Model Usage Guidelines
-----------------------------------
Employees may use approved AI tools including GPT-4 via internal API gateway only.
Direct OpenAI API access requires IT approval.
All AI-generated content must be reviewed by a human before publication.

Section 3: Incident Response
------------------------------
Any AI system producing harmful, biased, or incorrect outputs must be reported within 24 hours.
Contact the AI Governance team at ai-governance@company.com.
Systems producing critical errors must be taken offline immediately pending review.

Section 4: Approved Use Cases
-------------------------------
- Customer support chatbots (with human escalation path)
- Internal document search and summarization
- Code review assistance
- Data classification and tagging

Section 5: Prohibited Use Cases
---------------------------------
- Autonomous financial decision-making without human approval
- Facial recognition in public spaces
- Employee performance scoring without HR oversight
- Generating content that could be mistaken for official communications
